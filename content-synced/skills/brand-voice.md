# GSL Brand Voice

*Full body coming in week 3.* For now, the surface conventions live inline inside each playbook's audit prompt. See `docs/decisions/007-audit-scope-week3.md` for the sequencing.

In week 3, this file grows into the single source for:
- British English rules (always) and the short list of American spellings we catch most often
- No em-dash, no banned AI words, no "Hello, [Name]!" openers
- Indian context defaults (names, cities, scenarios, currency)
- The voice, warm but not cartoonish, confident but not corporate
- Sentence-case titles, active voice, "we" and "you" over passive constructions

Any playbook audit prompt that currently inlines these conventions will be refactored in week 3 to reference this skill by slug.

# GSL PPT Patterns

*Full body coming in week 3.* For now, the per-deck conventions live inline inside each PPT-producing playbook's audit prompt. See `docs/decisions/007-audit-scope-week3.md`.

In week 3, this file grows into the single source for:
- Title slide, section divider, content slide, closing slide layouts
- Sentence-case titles, never Title Case or ALL CAPS
- One big idea per slide, maximum 5 bullets, 8 words per bullet where possible
- 18pt minimum body text, 32pt+ headline
- GSL palette usage on slides, Turquoise and Azure as the duo
- Teacher notes filled in on every content slide
- Citation format for NCERT and external sources
- Safe zones and progress tabs for programme decks (VideoGenX, STEAM, Cambridge)

Until then, playbook audit prompts that produce decks inline the "one idea per slide, 5 bullets max, 18pt minimum, sentence case" rules as literal text in the audit.
